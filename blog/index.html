Brand and Logo Design

Saturday, April 5, 2014

The Hunt + Gather design group of Edmonton helped organize an fantastic Logo and Brand Workshop put on by Aaron Draplin of Draplin Design Co. I made some rough notes during the session on Draplin's process, techniques, and advice. Here's my take on some of those principles summarized.

Brand Design

Brands and logos should be tight, clean, simple, and recognizable. Symmetry and clean geometric shapes are help immensely in this regard. The typeface if any and colors you use have to fit the feel and essence you are going for. On that same tangent, considering your space and your competitors is essential in order to solidify your own brand.
